We know we’ve been pretty quiet since production wrapped — but trust us, things are moving steadily behind the scenes.

After spending the past few months digging through all the footage and picking out the most compelling material for the story, post-production is now fully underway. It’s an ongoing process of highs and lows, so bear with us and our little passion project 💛

While reviewing and playing around with the footage, we also got the chance to relive some really lovely (and sometimes very silly) moments from our filming days. Honestly, they made the whole process a lot more fun. There’s no way we could share everything, but we’ve put together a short couple-of-minutes blooper video to give you a taste of that side of things.

We first shared these bloopers with our Indiegogo backers who supported us with the “Q&A with Cast and Creators” perk. On April 18th, we had a really nice Q&A session with them and gave them early access — and now the video is finally out for everyone.

You can find it on our Instagram and on [@zoesthesia YouTube](https://youtu.be/I0uOc3Lgwv0?si=MGeJmSiLJlrtWuPl) (linked on our homepage as well). Go have a watch! All ad revenue from this video will go directly towards funding the documentary.

We’re also really happy to say that all the rewards for our “Your Name in Colours” and “Your Song in Shapes and Colours” backers have finally been delivered. We hope they’ll be a lasting little keepsake of this journey for everyone who supported us.

That’s all for now — we’ll be going quiet again for a bit as we dive back into the edit. We wish we were already at the point of sharing the finished film with you, but it still needs a bit more time (and patience!).

Speak soon ✨
